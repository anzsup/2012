Accommodation

Summer school attendees are responsible for securing their own accommodation. Here are a few options which provide easy access to the venue.

The Glenferrie Hotel

The Glenferrie Hotel is just across the road from the Hawthorn campus of SUT and offers a higher standard of accommodation but at a higher price than the other options listed. This would be appropriate accommodation for attendees travelling with family.

Website: http://www.glenferriehotel.com.au/accommodation/

On-campus

Swinburne Place Apartments

Brand new fully furnished multishare apartments conveniently located on campus (includes utilities and basic bed linen).

Approximate rate: $294 per week.

24 Wakefield Street, Hawthorn, Vic. 3122
Phone: +61-3-9914-2900
Email: swinburne.place@unilodge.com.au
Website: www.swinburneplace.com.au

Swinburne Student Apartments

Single room in a 2 or 3 bedroom furnished apartment with kitchen and bathroom.

Approximate rate: $222–$268 per week.

Phone: +61-3-9214-5555
Email: residences@swin.edu.au
Website: www.swin.edu.au/stuserv/residential

Other options
